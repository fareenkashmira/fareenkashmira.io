Technology

What is NFC?

Near Field Communication (NFC) lets two devices share data the instant they touch. One tap from a phone to an NFC-enabled card or tag and your full digital profile opens instantly — no app, no typing, no friction.

01
Tap to open Bring a phone within 4 cm of the NFC chip — your virtual card opens in the browser in under a second.
02
No app needed Works natively on all modern Android and iOS devices. Nothing to download, nothing to configure.
03
Always up to date The chip links to your live URL. Change your details anytime — the physical card stays the same.
04
Works everywhere Business cards, restaurant menus, hotel directories, product labels — NFC embeds into almost any material.
