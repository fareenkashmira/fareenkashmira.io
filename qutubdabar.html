Delhi Heritage

Delhi Darbar Classics

From the lanes of Purani Dilli to your table

Dilli ki Nihari
Slow-cooked overnight lamb stew with bone marrow, crowned with ginger julienne and fresh coriander. A Shahjahanabad legacy.
Mutton Burrah Kebab
Tender ribs marinated in mace, nutmeg and royal spices, charcoal-roasted. The pride of Jama Masjid.
Aloo Tikki Chaat
Crispy potato medallions layered with spiced chickpeas, tamarind chutney, yogurt and pomegranate. Straight from Chandni Chowk.
Butter Chicken Masala
Tandoori chicken simmered in tomato-cashew gravy enriched with butter and fenugreek. Born in Daryaganj, 1950s.
Tunday Kababi
Melt-in-mouth minced mutton patties with 160 secret spices. A Lucknow tradition reimagined for Delhi palates.
Chole Bhature
Fluffy deep-fried bread with spiced chickpea curry, pickled onions and green chutney. The ultimate Delhi breakfast.

Chilled saffron-almond milk garnished with silver leaf and pistachios. Royal sustenance.
Nimbu Pani Pudina
Fresh lime water with mint and black salt, served ice-cold in traditional clay glass.
Cold Coffee Shake
Blended coffee with vanilla ice cream and chocolate shavings. Modern Delhi café culture.
